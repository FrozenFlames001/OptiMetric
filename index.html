The Problem: The Precision Gap

Precision manufacturing currently relies on industrial-grade Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) that require high capital investment and specialized labor. For Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), this cost is a barrier to entry, forcing reliance on manual, error-prone measurement processes that increase waste and slow production.

The Solution: Democratized Quality Control

Our project introduces a Non-Contact, Automated Inspection Framework that transforms a standard smartphone into a high-precision measurement tool. By combining a 3D-printed standardized inspection rig with advanced Computer Vision (CV), we enable real-time, on-site tolerance checking.

About Us

Our system offers an affordable measurement solution for MSMEs by turning everyday smartphones into accurate inspection tools. Using technologies like OpenCV and LiDAR, the application analyzes object contours in real time and checks whether they meet required tolerances. This non-contact approach helps close the gap between costly industrial equipment and easy-to-use, decentralized quality control.
